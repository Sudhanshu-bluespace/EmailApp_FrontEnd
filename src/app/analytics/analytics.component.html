Campaign Wise Performance Statistics

This view allows you to analyze the campaign statistics completed by you till date. It has a stacked column chart with Total Reach, Clicks and Unsubscribes for each campaign.

Group Wise Unsubscription

This is a view of how your contacts have opted for unsubscribing from your campaigns. It shows a bar chart for number of unsubscribes per group that you have created.
